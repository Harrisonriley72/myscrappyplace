Fun With Creative Memories Peekaboo Pockets

Creative Memories Peekaboo Pockets are so much fun to use and can serve many purposes. They're great for adding some interaction to your pages and give you the ability to add more photos to a layout without having to add additional pages to your album. You know those two page layouts that are packed full of photos from that special day or event...but the photos are ALL so good that you MUST put them in the album? This is a great time to use a Peekaboo pocket. You can attach the Peekaboo on top of the page protector right over another photo of the same size, and add a spot for two more photos with just one Peekaboo! You can also add memorabilia or journaling to an already full layout. The CM Peekaboos come in a variety of sizes (4x6, 5x7, 4x4, 3x4, and 6x12). Give them a try and see what creative ways you can find to use them in your albums. You can find out more in my short demo video and this video where I did a "waterfall" of Peekaboos.
