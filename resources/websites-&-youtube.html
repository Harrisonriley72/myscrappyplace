Creative Memories blog

Follow the CM blog for excellent page layout ideas and sketches. Use the search box or categories drop down bar to find ideas for particular tools, collections, or occasions you want to scrap!

Creative Memories Youtube Channel

Watch Fast & Fun projects with Noreen every Wednesday at 5 pm central LIVE or watch the replays at your convenience. Noreen also has Scrapbooking 101 videos, which are very helpful for beginners and long-time scrappers as well.

Craft Some Joy

Lauren Hinds is an Independent Creative Memories advisor who has tons of helpful videos including "Tidy Up Tuesdays" episodes regarding organization of your scrapbook supplies and space, and "Friday Night Scrapbooking." She is definitely my favorite go to for ideas next to Noreen Smith! Sometimes I stream her videos while I'm scrapping and just watch and listen a bit and sometimes I build borders or scrap along with her. She is always inspiring and fun to watch!

Organized & Creative Mom

This YouTube channel is Noreen Smith's older stuff from before she went to work for Creative Memories home office. As always, she has so many great tips and ideas. Whenever you are stuck and need some inspiration, check out her videos, old or new!

Create with Krislyn

Krislyn is an Independent Creative Memories Advisor and blog contributor for Creative Memories. She makes beautiful layouts and has excellent step by step instruction videos. See her YouTube channel here.

Meggan Jacks Scrapbooking

Meggan is an Independent Creative Memories Advisor who posts regular videos where she demonstrates how to put together page layouts from Creative Memories sketches and recipes (many come from the CM blog).
